What does TourWithMe do?

TourWithMe is a platform for peer to peer tours, acitivites and experiences. It is where travelers meet locals, and locals can meet other locals.

I want to list a tour, what should I do?

You can list a tour by downloading the app (either from the App store or Google Play), and logging in. Once you are logged in, the app will help guide you through this process.

How do I get paid if I provide tours?

We will paypal or mail a check to you, after the tour is completed. You can select your preferred method in the app's "Earning" section.

I want to book a tour, what should I do?

You can book a tour by downloading the app (either from the App store or Google Play), and loggin in. Once you are logged in, you will be able to search for tours by topic, or geographic region (for example, you can easily find tours listed near you).

More questions?

We'd love to hear from you! Please write to us at hello@cometourwithme.com or live chat within the app to customer support at anytime.
